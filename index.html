Recipe's

Seafood Platter

Prawns and fish are cleaned and lightly seasoned, then grilled. Crab is steamed to preserve sweetness, and vegetables are blanched and finished with olive oil.

Herb Roasted Bread & Meatballs

Beef is mixed with herbs and spices, rolled into meatballs, and oven-roasted. Bread is baked until crisp and served warm. ($1)

Egg & Chicken Salad

Chicken is pan-seared and sliced. Eggs are fried until set, then combined with fresh lettuce and cucumber.

Fresh Vegetable Prep

Mushrooms are sliced and lightly sautéed, tomatoes are chopped fresh, and greens are washed and prepared with lemon.

Classic Pepperoni Pizza

Dough is rolled and topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and pepperoni, then baked at high heat until golden.

Mixed Olive Side

Olives are marinated with herbs and olive oil and lightly warmed before serving.
